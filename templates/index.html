What We Offer

A Complete Clinical Intelligence Platform

Combining genetic biomarkers, cognitive assessments, and lifestyle data to deliver accurate, personalized dementia risk predictions.

Subject & Visit Tracking

Each patient (Subject ID) may have multiple MRI visits over time, allowing longitudinal tracking of cognitive progression from Nondemented → Converted → Demented.

MRI Brain Volume Analysis

Uses eTIV (estimated Total Intracranial Volume), nWBV (normalized Whole Brain Volume), and ASF (Atlas Scaling Factor) extracted from OASIS longitudinal MRI scans.

Cognitive Scoring (MMSE & CDR)

Mini-Mental State Examination (MMSE) and Clinical Dementia Rating (CDR) scores are the core clinical indicators of cognitive decline used by the prediction model.

Demographic Features

Patient age, gender (M/F), dominant hand, years of education (EDUC), and socioeconomic status (SES) all contribute to the personalised risk assessment.

Multi-Model Comparison

Transparently compares Random Forest (70%), SVM (69%), and Logistic Regression (68%) — giving a clear view of algorithm strengths on real longitudinal MRI data.

OASIS Longitudinal Dataset

Trained on the publicly available OASIS-2 dataset: 1,210 MRI records from 150+ subjects aged 60–96, spanning Nondemented (602), Demented (461), and Converted (147) groups.

Process

How It Works

From patient data to a reliable prediction in four clear steps.

1
Register & Enter Data

Create an account and input your clinical profile — age, gender, genetic markers, lifestyle habits, and cognitive scores.

2
Data Preprocessing

The system cleans, encodes, and normalises your inputs, removing nulls and transforming categorical variables for the ML pipeline.

3

Your preprocessed data is passed through the trained Random Forest model, with features weighted by clinical importance.

4

A clear dementia risk prediction (positive / negative) is returned alongside the confidence score and key contributing factors.
